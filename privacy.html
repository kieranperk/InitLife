InitLife Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

InitLife is designed as a local-first personal interface. The majority of your data stays on your device and is stored in your browser’s local storage. We do not operate a backend database and do not collect or store personal user data on external servers.

Data Storage

All user preferences such as your name, settings, and local configuration are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly connect third-party services.

Google Account Integration

If you choose to connect a Google account, InitLife requests limited read-only access to:

  • Google Calendar (read-only events)
  • Gmail (read-only unread message count)
  • Google Contacts (read-only birthday data)
  • Basic profile information (first name only)

Authentication is handled directly via Google OAuth. Access tokens are stored locally in your browser and are never transmitted to any external server controlled by InitLife.

You can revoke access at any time via your Google Account security settings.

Location Data

If you allow location access, your device’s GPS coordinates are used only to display a localised location name. Reverse geocoding is performed using OpenStreetMap’s Nominatim service. Coordinates are not stored on any server operated by InitLife.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transmit your personal data to third parties. No analytics platforms, tracking scripts, or advertising networks are used.

Third-Party Services

InitLife relies on the following external services when enabled by the user:

  • Google APIs (Calendar, Gmail, Contacts)
  • Google OAuth authentication
  • OpenStreetMap Nominatim (reverse geocoding)

These services are governed by their own privacy policies.

Cookies

InitLife does not use cookies for tracking. Only browser local storage is used to persist settings.

Data Control

You can delete all stored data at any time by using the “reset” function inside the application, which clears local storage on your device.

Security

Because all sensitive data remains client-side, security is dependent on your device and browser. We recommend using a secure device and keeping your browser up to date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact the developer via the project repository or website contact method (if available).

This policy applies only to the InitLife web application and not to any third-party services it may interact with.
